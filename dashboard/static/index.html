The Agent Matrix

data_provider

Fetches price + option chains (±3 strikes), VIX, and historical bars. Falls back to a self-contained Black-Scholes engine + mock executor when market credentials are absent.

analyst

Flags BEAR regime when VIX > 20.50. Screens by IV velocity vs. prior snapshot and Call/Put ratio ≥ 1.10, ranks candidates, and proposes structured option legs.

evaluator

Guardrail LLM. If confidence < 0.70 it routes back to the analyst (up to 3 reflections); otherwise it proceeds to risk.

risk_manager

Enforces 2% position sizing, ±1000 portfolio-delta limit, ≥30% credit-of-width, and an 80% margin ceiling. Interrupts for human approval on breach.

execute_trade

Submits orders (or mock), computes slippage, and dispatches a daily summary report.

Safety & Reliability

Strict Pydantic schemas on every signal, Postgres-backed checkpointing for human-in-the-loop resume, and cost-conscious telemetry sampling.
